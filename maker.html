Biography

Shun Yonemura

Ocarina maker from Japan. Born in 1989 and raised in Tokyo.

2024 Started producing my own ocarina.
2021–2023 Worked at a manufacturer of western concert flute, making flute foot joints.
2020 Started making pottery.
2016–2019 Lived in Lugo to study in graduate shcool.
2014–2015 Visit Santiago de Compostela for the third time. There, I encountered the ocarina I had been searching for, and finally met the maker, Manuel Barreiro "Paparolo".
2010 First visit to Santiago de Compostela, Spain, to find the ocarina Carlos Núñez was using.
2006 Warched an animation film "Tales from Earthsea" in the theatre. After hearing Carlos Núñez's performance in the music, I became interested in the ocarina.
