SHUNARTESÁN

Handmade ocarina for the traditional music of Galicia and Ireland.

PRODUCT

The structure is designed inspiring by a Spanish artist, Mo. Manuel Barreiro (Paparolo). A single-chamber and a doble-hole for the right pinky. It enables rythmic playing with chromatic and also diatonic scales, for Muiñeira, Xota, Reel and Jig. Of couse, it is ideal for playing Alalá and Air as well.

They are durable and maintenable due to firing in higher temperture. The colors and textures are entirely created through glazing and firing, with no paints or coating applied. It can be treated just like tableware.

The nomenclature is based on the standard by Paparolo and the classificaton of the recorder.

ORIGINAL: 11 holes, 13 notes

The range is one note wider in the higher register without adding extra holes. It allows to perform more Irish pieces that previosly requierd a double-chamber.

Only the three notes in the higher register have different fingerings from the usual ones, but the rest follow the common fingering.

Lowest Highest
Soprano D C#5 A6
Soprano C B4 G6

BASIC: 11 holes, 12 notes
